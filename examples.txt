==============================================

Case 1 — Topic passes in a higher grade but fails in a lower grade
                   even after the one-shot refinement (retry cap reached)
------------------------------------------------------------------
The topic itself is beyond the lower grade's scope, so no amount of
vocabulary simplification can make it age-appropriate. The refined
output will still be flagged by the reviewer.

  Passes in higher grade:
    Grade: 9
    Topic: Mitochondria

  Fails in lower grade (expect fail -> refine -> still fail):
    Grade: 1
    Topic: Mitochondria

  Other good "hard fail" combos for lower grades:
    Grade: 2, Topic: Quadratic equations
    Grade: 1, Topic: Newton's laws of motion
    Grade: 2, Topic: Electromagnetic induction

------------------------------------------------------------------
Topic is appropriate for the grade, but the first draft usually slips
in vocabulary or concepts above the grade level. The reviewer flags
those issues; the generator re-runs with that feedback and produces a
simpler version that passes.

    Grade: 3
    Topic: Photosynthesis

  Other good "fail -> refine -> pass" combos:
    Grade: 4, Topic: The water cycle
    Grade: 5, Topic: Gravity

Notes
-----
- Results are probabilistic — the LLM is not fully deterministic even
  at low temperature. If a case behaves differently, run it again or
  try one of the alternatives listed.
- The retry cap is one refinement pass, matching the PDF spec.
